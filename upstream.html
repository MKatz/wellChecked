Upstream

WCSI provides last mile eyes on your remote unmanned assets. When you can only route a pumper to sites on a daily basis (up to two times a day) economically, WCSI visits the site every 30 minutes providing the same service as the pumper, but much more.

WCSI introduces EBM (Exception Based Monitoring) when conditions have changed, notifications via text and email with video linked, Cloud based storage for access on smartphones/tablets/laptops, and real time video access. WCSI is your on-site solution.

Now with local automation collection, WCSI translates protocol serial data into useable ASCII data in a three-tiered approach... breaking the two-tiered proprietary automation ventor solution.
